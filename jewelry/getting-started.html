Getting Started

let's get started by making a simple pair of earrings!

Tools and Materials Needed

2 head pins or eye pins
2 earring fishhooks
2 of your favorite beads
Round nose pliers (or a 3 in 1 tool in my case)
Wire cutters

Instructions

Step 1

take one of the eye pins/head pins and
insert it into the hole on one of your beads. If
you find that the hole in your bead is too
large to stay on, consider adding a smaller
bead to act as a buffer.

Step 2

Use the round nose pliers (or round part
of the 3 in 1 tool) to create a loop in the
wire opposite the eye/head of your pin.
Don’t close the loop.

Step 3

cut the excess wire with your wire
cutters (or the wire-cutting segment of
your 3 in 1 tool).

Step 4

hook the open loop of your eye/head
pin onto the earring fishhook.

Step 5

close the loop on your pin so it
won’t fall off.

Step 6

Repeat steps 1-5 to complete the second
earring.

Voila! Your first handmade jewelry piece!
