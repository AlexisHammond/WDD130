Let's Learn Jewelry Making

Making your own jewelry can seem intimidating
at first. My mission is to help introduce more
people to my personal craft of choice and
overcome some of the confusion and uncertainty
that comeswith learning a new hobby.

Years ago, craft stores such as Joann’s and Michaels
used to host in-person classes for their community to
learn new crafts and techniques. Nowadays, craft
stores don’t host these classes, and we are left to
scour the internet for instruction. Of course, there are
many other tutorials you can follow to learn how to
make jewelry with many other techniques, but I figure
I should share my knowledge and experience as well.

The purpose of this website is to share information
on the best places to buy supplies, what supplies to
get, the best brands, and show readers like you how
to get started. Soon, you’ll be wearing bracelets,
necklaces, and earrings that you’ve created yourself-
and you’ll have the joy of knowing that you made it. If
you are ready to begin your new hobby, simply click
on the button labeled “Tools & Materials”.
